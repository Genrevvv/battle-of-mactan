About us

"Retelling the Battle. Reclaiming the Legacy"

We are a student-led team dedicated to uncovering the truth behind the Battle of Mactan. Through research, design, and storytelling, we honor Lapu-Lapu and the warriors who shaped Philippine history—challenging myths, amplifying overlooked voices, and inspiring pride in our heritage.

Our goal is to uncover the truths behind the legend and explore the historical forces that shaped Filipino identity.

Information Gatherers Chlarenz Togueño, Jenny Lyn Roquid, Dave Melano, John Vincent Libao, Lara Casinillo — Researchers who dig deep into history to bring facts to light.

Coders Jheslee Merto, Kenneth Delacruz, Rm Vidallo, Genrev Aguilar, Hera Chelsy Thea Lo — Developers who turn knowledge into interactive digital experiences.

Actors / Voice Overs Demy Adrian, Moya James, Mareau Santos, Jayson Bustaleño ,Lawrence Mojica, Marcus Royce Matic, Sairo Orias, Si Chano ,Tristan Japay — Voices and faces that bring our narrative to life.
